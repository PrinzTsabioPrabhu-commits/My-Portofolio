MY DAILY ACTIVITIES AT IDN .



In IDN, first we wake up at 3.30 pm, and then prepare for tahjjud prayer. And then we do tilawah before subuh praer until adzan subuh was start. After that we do a Halaqoh Quran until 5.45am.

After that, we go back to dormitory to take a bath and breakfast for prepare before Morning spirit. After morning spirit, we go do class hour until Ashar.

After we do ashar prayer, we read evening dzikir and then play at yard until 5.00pm, after that we take a bath and diner before maghrib prayer, after do maghrib prayer, we do Halaqoh Quran again until isya prayer time. Then do a Isya prayer and go to dormitory for dormitory time, after that, we go back to sleep.
